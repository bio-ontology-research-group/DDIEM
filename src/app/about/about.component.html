DDIEM - Drug database for inborn error of metabolism


Welcome to DDIEM, Drug database for inborn errors of metabolism. DDIEM database is a database on therapeutic strategies for inborn errors of metabolism. These strategies are classified by mechanism and outcome in . DDIEM use this ontology to structure the experimental treatments that have been proposed or applied. It includes description of phenotypes addressed by the treatment, drugs participated in treatment and procedures. DDIEM is jointly developed by researchers from and the .
